StudyPod is the first online platform that promotes social interaction among peers while at the same time improving educational growth! StudyPod provides students with the ability to connect with other students from their neighborhood and school outside of the classroom and form study groups from the safety of their homes- a huge plus especially in these times! Students can benefit from accessing personalized and curated resources based on their academic and extra-curricular interests. StudyPod truly is the first online network where students can learn collaboratively, share and thrive beyond the classroom.
