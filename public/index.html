Why Choose StudyPod?

Unlike traditional tutoring apps which promise to make your kid succeed at expensive costs, StudyPod offers a low-cost solution-students helping students! StudyPod enables students to connect to their classmates outside the classroom, form study groups and ask difficult questions which may have been overlooked in class, all from the safety of their homes!

Benefits

  • Form study groups and break down difficult questions with your peers!
  • Find top resources any topic or subject matter you need more help with
  • Ask questions to your peers

  • Connect with your classmates outside the classroom!
